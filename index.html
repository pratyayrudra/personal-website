Few Words About Me

I started my coding journey when I was 14 years old with C++. In the coming years, I learned other languages like Python, C, JAVA.

Along with programming, I got interested in Web Development and started to learn HTML, CSS, JavaScript. Then I moved to frameworks like Bootstrap. After getting a grip on Front-end development, I started exploring Back-end development. So NodeJS, MongoDB, Mongoose, ExpressJS were the first things I learned.

Simultaneously with Full Stack, I even mastered a CMS like WordPress. I did four internships during my college on WordPress development and made several websites. After working on many real-world projects, I understood the importance of Server and Cloud.

I started exploring and ended up with Andrew Brown tutorials on AWS. So I started my journey with AWS, then GCP, and finally Oracle Cloud. In the future, I plan on sticking with AWS (Get Certified) and learn Azure. Along with that, I even learned Containerization, Docker, CI/CD (Github Actions), GIT Deployments, and much more.

I am still learning new technologies every day and continue to expand my skill set.
Currently, I am a final year B.Tech (Information Technology) student, will be graduating in 2021. I am looking for a Career in Cloud or Full Stack Development. For any opportunities, or offer you can contact me.
